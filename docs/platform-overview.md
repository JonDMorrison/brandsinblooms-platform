# Platform Overview

## Introduction
The Brands In Blooms platform is a modern, scalable, multi-tenant system designed for non-technical users. It integrates AI-powered site generation, dropshipping e-commerce, and an intuitive CMS to enable site owners to manage websites effortlessly, customers to shop seamlessly, and admins to oversee operations globally. Simplicity is core, reducing friction across all interactions.

## User Types and Experiences
### Site Owner
Manages individual sites via a personal dashboard: AI-assisted content creation, product selection from a central catalogue, order tracking, and profile management. Focus on drag-and-drop tools and minimal steps.

### Customer
Browses site content and products, adds to cart, checks out via Stripe (guest or saved data), receives email updates. Responsive design ensures easy navigation and quick purchases.

### Admin
Accesses a super dashboard for global oversight: Manage any site, view sales analytics, handle users. Simple search and one-click actions for efficiency.

## Integrated Features
- **AI Integration**: Automates site setup and content generation for owners and admins.
- **E-commerce**: "Endless aisle" catalogue with Stripe payments, drop-shipping.
- **Multi-tenancy**: Secure separation of sites with custom domains.
- **Analytics**: Basic reporting for admins and owners.

## Overall Flow
Site owners build and maintain sites using AI and CMS tools. Customers interact with these sites for browsing and buying. Admins monitor and support the ecosystem. All experiences prioritize intuitive interfaces, mobile responsiveness, and quick task completion to drive growth and retention.
