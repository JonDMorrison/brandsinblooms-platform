# Customer Experience

## Overview
Customers are visitors to a Site Owner's website who browse content and explore in-store product offerings. The platform ensures a modern, engaging experience that showcases the garden center's inventory and drives foot traffic to their physical location. No technical knowledge required; focus on intuitive navigation and discovery.

## Key Features
- **Browsing Content**: View site pages, blog entries, events calendar, and store information. Clean, responsive design for all devices.
- **In-Store Product Discovery**: Explore products available at the physical garden center with search, filters, and detailed information.
- **Store Information**: Easy access to location, hours, directions, and special announcements.
- **Contact and Inquiry**: Simple forms to ask about product availability or general questions.
- **Events and Workshops**: Browse upcoming garden workshops, seasonal events, and educational content.

## User Journeys
### Product Discovery and Store Visit
1. Land on site homepage featuring seasonal highlights.
2. Browse in-store product catalog or search for specific plants/supplies.
3. View product details, care instructions, and availability notes.
4. Check store hours and get directions.
5. Visit physical location to purchase.

### Event Registration
1. Navigate to events section.
2. View upcoming workshops or seasonal events.
3. Register interest or RSVP through simple form.
4. Receive confirmation and reminder emails.

### Product Inquiry
1. Browse product catalog.
2. Use "Ask About This Product" feature.
3. Submit inquiry about availability, sizing, or care.
4. Receive response from garden center staff.

### Information Seeking
1. Read blog posts about gardening tips.
2. View seasonal planting guides.
3. Access care instructions for specific plants.
4. Find store contact information easily.

## Navigation Structure
- Homepage: Seasonal features, store highlights, quick links.
- In-Store Catalog: Categories, search bar, product showcase.
- About Us: Store story, team, mission.
- Visit Us: Location, hours, directions, parking info.
- Events: Calendar of workshops and seasonal activities.
- Blog: Gardening tips, seasonal advice, plant care guides.
- Contact: Inquiry forms, phone, email.

## Key Design Principles
- **Visual Focus**: Beautiful plant photography and garden inspiration.
- **Local Connection**: Emphasize the personal, local garden center experience.
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for customers checking info on-the-go.
- **Quick Access**: Store hours and location always visible.
- **Inspiring Content**: Showcase what's possible in their garden.

Emphasis on mobile-friendly design, clear calls-to-action to visit the store, and engaging content that builds trust and drives foot traffic to the physical location.
